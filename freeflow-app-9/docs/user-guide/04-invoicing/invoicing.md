# Invoicing & Payments

Create professional invoices and get paid faster with KAZI's comprehensive billing features.

## Creating Invoices

### New Invoice
1. Go to **Invoices**
2. Click **+ New Invoice**
3. Complete the invoice:

#### Client Details
- Select client (auto-fills address)
- Add billing contact if different
- Choose currency

#### Invoice Information
- **Invoice Number**: Auto-generated or custom
- **Invoice Date**: When issued
- **Due Date**: Payment deadline
- **Reference**: PO number or reference

#### Line Items
**From Time Entries:**
1. Click **Import Time**
2. Select date range
3. Choose entries to include
4. Time converts to line items

**Manual Items:**
1. Click **+ Add Item**
2. Enter description
3. Set quantity and rate
4. Apply tax if needed

#### Totals
- Subtotal (auto-calculated)
- Tax (configurable)
- Discounts (percentage or fixed)
- Total amount due

### Invoice Preview
- Click **Preview** before sending
- Check formatting
- Verify all details
- Test PDF download

## Invoice Templates

### Customizing Templates
1. Go to **Settings > Invoice Templates**
2. Choose template style
3. Customize:
   - Logo and branding
   - Colors
   - Header/footer text
   - Payment terms
   - Notes section

### Template Elements
- **Header**: Logo, business name, contact
- **Client Block**: Client details
- **Items Table**: Services/products
- **Totals**: Subtotal, tax, total
- **Footer**: Payment instructions, terms

## Sending Invoices

### Email Invoice
1. Click **Send Invoice**
2. Review email content
3. Customize message
4. Add CC recipients
5. Click **Send**

### Download PDF
- Click **Download PDF**
- Share manually
- Print for records

### Client Portal
- Invoice appears in client's portal
- They can view and pay online
- Status updates automatically

## Payment Methods

### Setting Up Payments

#### Stripe
1. Go to **Settings > Payments**
2. Click **Connect Stripe**
3. Complete Stripe onboarding
4. Accept card payments

#### PayPal
1. Click **Connect PayPal**
2. Log into PayPal
3. Authorize KAZI
4. Ready for PayPal payments

#### Crypto Payments
1. Click **Enable Crypto**
2. Connect wallet
3. Accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.

#### Bank Transfer
1. Add bank details
2. Include in invoice
3. Mark paid manually

### Payment Links
- Each invoice has a unique payment link
- Clients click to pay
- Multiple payment options shown

## Invoice Status

### Status Types
- **Draft**: Not yet sent
- **Sent**: Delivered to client
- **Viewed**: Client opened invoice
- **Paid**: Payment received
- **Overdue**: Past due date
- **Cancelled**: Voided

### Tracking Payments
- View payment history
- Partial payment support
- Payment confirmation emails

## Recurring Invoices

### Setup
1. Go to **Invoices > Recurring**
2. Click **+ New Recurring**
3. Configure:
   - Client
   - Amount
   - Frequency (weekly, monthly, etc.)
   - Start date
   - End date (optional)

### Management
- View all recurring schedules
- Pause/resume
- Edit upcoming invoices
- Cancel recurring

## Payment Reminders

### Automatic Reminders
1. Go to **Settings > Payment Reminders**
2. Enable automatic reminders
3. Configure schedule:
   - 3 days before due
   - On due date
   - 7 days overdue
   - 14 days overdue

### Manual Reminders
- Click **Send Reminder** on any invoice
- Customize reminder message
- Track reminder history

## Late Fees

### Configuration
1. Go to **Settings > Late Fees**
2. Enable late fees
3. Set fee structure:
   - Percentage of total
   - Fixed amount
   - Grace period

### Application
- Auto-applied after grace period
- Shown on updated invoice
- Client notification sent

## Tax Management

### Tax Rates
1. Go to **Settings > Taxes**
2. Add tax rates:
   - Name (e.g., "VAT", "GST")
   - Percentage
   - Default or per-item

### Tax Reports
- View tax collected
- Export for filing
- Track by period

## Expense Tracking

### Adding Expenses
1. Go to **Invoices > Expenses**
2. Click **+ Add Expense**
3. Enter:
   - Description
   - Amount
   - Category
   - Receipt (upload)
   - Project link

### Billable Expenses
- Mark expenses as billable
- Add to client invoices
- Apply markup if desired

## Reports

### Invoice Reports
- **Revenue Report**: Total invoiced
- **Payment Report**: Received payments
- **Aging Report**: Outstanding invoices
- **Client Report**: Per-client billing

### Export
- Download as PDF/CSV
- Date range selection
- Filter by client/status

## Best Practices

### Invoice Creation
1. Send invoices promptly
2. Include clear descriptions
3. Set reasonable due dates
4. Double-check amounts

### Getting Paid
1. Enable multiple payment methods
2. Use automatic reminders
3. Follow up personally when needed
4. Offer early payment discounts

### Record Keeping
1. Keep all invoices organized
2. Archive paid invoices
3. Back up regularly
4. Maintain tax records
