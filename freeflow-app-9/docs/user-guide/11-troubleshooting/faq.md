# Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### What is KAZI?
KAZI is an all-in-one freelance business management platform that combines project management, time tracking, invoicing, client communication, and AI-powered tools in one place.

### Is KAZI free?
KAZI offers a free tier with basic features. Premium plans unlock advanced features like unlimited projects, AI tools, and priority support. See our [pricing page](/pricing) for details.

### What devices can I use KAZI on?
KAZI works on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. We also offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android.

### How do I get started?
1. Sign up at kazi.com
2. Complete your profile
3. Add your first client
4. Create a project
5. Start tracking time!

## Account & Billing

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to **Settings > Billing** and click "Upgrade Plan". Choose your desired plan and complete payment.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable but you can cancel anytime.

### How do I export my data?
Go to **Settings > Data Export** to download all your data in CSV or JSON format.

### Can I have multiple workspaces?
Yes, Business and Enterprise plans support multiple workspaces with separate clients and projects.

## Projects & Tasks

### How many projects can I create?
Free tier: 3 active projects. Pro tier: Unlimited. All archived projects don't count toward limits.

### Can clients see my projects?
Only if you enable the Client Portal for that project. You control what clients can see.

### How do I archive a project?
Open the project, click **Settings**, then "Archive Project". Archived projects are hidden but preserved.

### Can I duplicate a project?
Yes! Click the **...** menu on any project and select "Duplicate". Great for similar client work.

### How do templates work?
Templates let you save project structures (tasks, milestones) to reuse. Create them in Settings > Project Templates.

## Time Tracking

### Does the timer work offline?
The browser extension works offline and syncs when you reconnect. The web app requires an internet connection.

### Can I track time for past dates?
Yes, use Manual Entry to log time for any date. Go to Time Tracking > Manual Entry.

### How accurate is the timer?
The timer tracks to the second. For billing, time is typically rounded to the nearest minute or specified interval.

### Can team members see each other's time?
Depends on permissions. Admins see all time. Team members see only their own unless granted viewer access.

### What happens if I forget to stop the timer?
KAZI detects idle time. You can set it to pause automatically, discard idle time, or prompt you.

## Invoicing & Payments

### What payment methods do clients have?
Clients can pay via credit/debit card (Stripe), PayPal, bank transfer, or cryptocurrency (if enabled).

### When do I receive payments?
Card payments: 2-3 business days. PayPal: Instant. Bank transfers: 3-5 business days.

### Can I use my own invoice numbering?
Yes! Go to **Settings > Invoice Settings** and customize the numbering format.

### Are invoices legally compliant?
KAZI invoices include all standard fields. Add your tax ID and comply with local requirements as needed.

### Can I send invoices in different currencies?
Yes, select the currency when creating an invoice. Conversion rates are updated daily.

## Team & Collaboration

### How do I invite team members?
Go to **Team > Invite** and enter their email. They'll receive an invitation to join.

### What are the different roles?
- **Admin**: Full access, manage team
- **Manager**: Project & team management
- **Member**: Work on assigned projects
- **Viewer**: Read-only access

### Can team members see client information?
Based on permissions. You can restrict access to specific projects or clients.

### Is there a limit on team size?
Free: 1 user. Pro: 5 users. Business: 25 users. Enterprise: Unlimited.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use 256-bit encryption, secure data centers, and regular security audits. We're SOC 2 compliant.

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in secure AWS data centers. EU customers' data stays in EU regions.

### Can I enable 2FA?
Yes, go to **Settings > Security** and enable Two-Factor Authentication.

### Does KAZI sell my data?
No. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

### How long is my data retained?
Active accounts: Forever. Deleted accounts: 30 days (then permanently removed).
