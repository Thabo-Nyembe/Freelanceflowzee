# Video Tutorial Script: Invoicing Walkthrough

**Duration**: 6 minutes
**Target Audience**: Users ready to invoice clients
**Goal**: Complete invoice creation to payment receipt

---

## INTRO (0:00 - 0:20)

**[SCREEN: Invoice dashboard]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Getting paid should be easy. In this tutorial, I'll show you how to create professional invoices in KAZI, send them to clients, and get paid - all in just a few clicks."

---

## CREATING AN INVOICE (0:20 - 2:00)

### Starting Fresh (0:20 - 0:45)

**[SCREEN: Invoices page]**

**NARRATOR**:
"From your dashboard, click Invoices in the sidebar. Then click 'New Invoice'."

**[CURSOR: Navigate and click new invoice]**

### Basic Details (0:45 - 1:15)

**[SCREEN: Invoice creation form]**

**NARRATOR**:
"KAZI auto-generates an invoice number, but you can customize it. Select your client from the dropdown - their billing info fills in automatically."

**[CURSOR: Select client]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Set the invoice date and due date. Net 30 is standard, but you can choose whatever terms you've agreed with your client."

### Adding Line Items (1:15 - 2:00)

**NARRATOR**:
"Now for the billable items. You have two options:"

**[CURSOR: Click Import Time]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Option one: Import tracked time. Click 'Import Time', select the date range, and your time entries appear. Check the ones to include, and they convert to line items automatically."

**[SCREEN: Time import flow]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Option two: Add manual items. Click 'Add Item', enter a description, quantity, and rate. Great for fixed-price work or expenses."

**[CURSOR: Add manual item]**

---

## CUSTOMIZING THE INVOICE (2:00 - 3:00)

### Discounts & Tax (2:00 - 2:30)

**[SCREEN: Invoice totals section]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Need to add a discount? Click 'Add Discount' and enter a percentage or fixed amount. Your total updates instantly."

**[CURSOR: Add discount]**

**NARRATOR**:
"For tax, select your tax rate from the dropdown. KAZI handles the math and displays it clearly for your client."

### Notes & Terms (2:30 - 3:00)

**NARRATOR**:
"Scroll down to add a personal note - thanking them for their business, for example. And review the payment terms shown at the bottom."

**[CURSOR: Add note]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Everything look good? Click 'Preview' to see exactly what your client will receive."

**[SCREEN: Invoice preview]**

---

## SENDING THE INVOICE (3:00 - 3:45)

**[SCREEN: Send invoice dialog]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Ready to send? Click 'Send Invoice'. KAZI composes a professional email with your invoice attached and a pay button."

**[CURSOR: Click send]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Customize the message if you want, add CC recipients, and click Send."

**[SCREEN: Sending animation]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Done! Your invoice is on its way. The status changes to 'Sent'."

**[SCREEN: Invoice list showing 'Sent' status]**

---

## CLIENT PAYMENT EXPERIENCE (3:45 - 4:30)

**[SCREEN: Client's email view]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Here's what your client sees. They get a clean email with the invoice summary and a big 'Pay Now' button."

**[SCREEN: Click pay now]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Clicking Pay Now takes them to a secure payment page. They can pay with:
- Credit or debit card
- PayPal
- Bank transfer
- Or crypto, if you've enabled it"

**[SCREEN: Payment options]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Once they complete payment, both of you get a confirmation email."

---

## TRACKING PAYMENTS (4:30 - 5:15)

**[SCREEN: Invoice detail with payment]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Back in KAZI, your invoice automatically updates to 'Paid'. You can see:
- When the payment was made
- Which method was used
- The transaction ID for your records"

**[CURSOR: Highlight payment details]**

### Partial Payments

**NARRATOR**:
"KAZI also handles partial payments. If a client pays half now and half later, the invoice shows the remaining balance."

### Overdue Invoices

**[SCREEN: Overdue invoice]**

**NARRATOR**:
"For overdue invoices, KAZI can send automatic reminders. Or click 'Send Reminder' to nudge them manually."

---

## PRO TIPS (5:15 - 5:45)

**[SCREEN: Invoice settings]**

**NARRATOR**:
"A few pro tips to get paid faster:"

**NARRATOR**:
"One - Enable automatic payment reminders in Settings. KAZI will follow up for you."

**NARRATOR**:
"Two - Offer multiple payment methods. The easier you make it, the faster they pay."

**NARRATOR**:
"Three - Use recurring invoices for retainer clients. Set it once, and invoices go out automatically each month."

---

## WRAP UP (5:45 - 6:00)

**[SCREEN: Happy dashboard with paid invoices]**

**NARRATOR**:
"That's invoicing in KAZI - create, send, and get paid. Simple as that."

**NARRATOR**:
"Questions? Check our help center or reach out to support. Happy invoicing!"

**[SCREEN: KAZI logo]**
