# Video Tutorial Script: Getting Started

**Duration**: 10 minutes
**Target Audience**: New users completing setup
**Goal**: Guide users through complete account setup

---

## INTRO (0:00 - 0:30)

**[SCREEN: KAZI welcome screen]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Welcome to KAZI! In this tutorial, I'll walk you through setting up your account from scratch. By the end, you'll have your profile complete, your first client added, and your first project ready to go. Let's dive in."

---

## ACCOUNT SETUP (0:30 - 2:30)

### Profile Basics (0:30 - 1:30)

**[SCREEN: Profile settings page]**

**NARRATOR**:
"After signing up, your first stop is your profile. Click your avatar in the top right, then 'Profile Settings'."

**[CURSOR: Navigate to profile]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Fill in your basic info:
- Your name as you want clients to see it
- A professional profile photo
- Your job title - this appears on invoices
- Your location and timezone - important for scheduling"

**[CURSOR: Fill in fields]**

### Business Details (1:30 - 2:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"Scroll down to Business Information. This is what appears on your invoices and contracts."

**[CURSOR: Scroll and fill]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Add your:
- Business name if you have one
- Business address
- Tax ID or VAT number if applicable
- Default hourly rate - you can override this per project"

**NARRATOR**:
"Click Save, and your profile is ready."

---

## PAYMENT SETUP (2:30 - 4:00)

**[SCREEN: Payment settings]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Now let's set up how you get paid. Go to Settings, then Payment Methods."

**[CURSOR: Navigate to payment settings]**

### Connecting Stripe (2:45 - 3:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"Stripe is the easiest way to accept card payments. Click 'Connect Stripe'."

**[SCREEN: Stripe OAuth flow]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Sign in to your Stripe account - or create one, it's free. Authorize KAZI to process payments on your behalf."

**[CURSOR: Complete Stripe connection]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Once connected, clients can pay invoices instantly with credit or debit card. Payments land in your Stripe account within 2-3 days."

### Other Payment Options (3:30 - 4:00)

**NARRATOR**:
"You can also connect PayPal, add bank details for wire transfers, or enable cryptocurrency payments. Set up whatever your clients prefer."

---

## ADDING YOUR FIRST CLIENT (4:00 - 5:30)

**[SCREEN: Clients page]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Time to add your first client. Click Clients in the sidebar, then 'Add Client'."

**[CURSOR: Navigate and click]**

### Client Details (4:15 - 5:00)

**[SCREEN: New client form]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Fill in their information:
- Company name and contact person
- Email address - they'll receive invoices here
- Phone number and address for your records
- Their preferred currency"

**[CURSOR: Fill in demo client]**

### Client Portal (5:00 - 5:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"Enable the Client Portal to give your client their own login. They can view projects, approve milestones, and pay invoices. You control what they see."

**[CURSOR: Toggle client portal]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Click Save, and your client is ready."

---

## CREATING YOUR FIRST PROJECT (5:30 - 7:30)

**[SCREEN: Projects Hub]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Let's create a project for this client. Click Projects Hub, then 'New Project'."

**[CURSOR: Navigate and click]**

### Project Setup (5:45 - 6:30)

**[SCREEN: New project wizard]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Enter:
- A clear project name
- Select your client from the dropdown
- Add a description of the scope
- Set start and end dates"

**[CURSOR: Fill in project details]**

### Budget & Billing (6:30 - 7:00)

**NARRATOR**:
"Now the billing setup:
- Choose Hourly if you're billing by time, Fixed for a set price, or Retainer for monthly engagements
- Set a budget limit if you want to cap hours
- Enter the hourly rate for this project"

**[CURSOR: Configure billing]**

### Adding Tasks (7:00 - 7:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"Click Create, and your project is live. Now let's add some tasks."

**[SCREEN: Project board view]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Click 'Add Task', enter the task name, assign it to yourself, and set a due date. You can add subtasks, descriptions, and attachments later."

**[CURSOR: Create first tasks]**

---

## STARTING YOUR FIRST TIMER (7:30 - 8:30)

**[SCREEN: Time tracking]**

**NARRATOR**:
"Your project has tasks. Now let's track time. Go to Time Tracking."

**[CURSOR: Navigate to time tracking]**

### Using the Timer (7:45 - 8:15)

**NARRATOR**:
"Enter what you're working on in the description. Select your project. Click Start. That's it - you're tracking."

**[CURSOR: Start timer]**

**NARRATOR**:
"The timer appears in your top bar so you always know it's running. Click Stop when you're done, and the entry is saved automatically."

**[CURSOR: Stop timer]**

### Viewing Entries (8:15 - 8:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"Your time entries appear in the list below. You can edit, delete, or mark entries as billable or non-billable."

---

## CUSTOMIZATION TIPS (8:30 - 9:30)

**[SCREEN: Settings overview]**

**NARRATOR**:
"A few quick customizations to make KAZI yours:"

### Invoice Template (8:40 - 9:00)

**NARRATOR**:
"Go to Settings, Invoice Templates. Upload your logo, choose your color scheme, and customize the footer text. Your invoices will look professional from day one."

### Notifications (9:00 - 9:15)

**NARRATOR**:
"Under Notifications, choose what you want to be alerted about - new payments, messages, task assignments. Set email frequency to avoid overload."

### Integrations (9:15 - 9:30)

**NARRATOR**:
"And check out Integrations to connect your calendar, Slack, or accounting software."

---

## WRAP UP (9:30 - 10:00)

**[SCREEN: Completed dashboard with data]**

**NARRATOR**:
"That's it! You've got:
- A complete profile
- Payment methods ready
- Your first client
- An active project with tasks
- And you know how to track time"

**NARRATOR**:
"You're ready to run your freelance business with KAZI. In our next video, we'll cover creating and sending your first invoice."

**[SCREEN: KAZI logo + links to other tutorials]**
