## About KAZI

KAZI is an AI-native, all-in-one platform that combines professional creative tools, complete business operations, and advanced AI capabilities for freelancers and creative professionals.

- Real-time collaboration

**Price: $49-99/month** - 50%+ savings

## FAQ for Press

**Q: How is KAZI different from HoneyBook or Bonsai?**
A: KAZI includes professional video editing tools and 12+ AI models that those platforms lack. We also have escrow payment protection, which is unique in this space.

**Q: Why should someone pay $49-99 when tools like Notion are cheaper?**
A: Notion doesn't include invoicing, video editing, or escrow payments. When you add up all the tools KAZI replaces (HoneyBook, Loom, Adobe, Slack, Canva, Notion), you're paying $100+/month. KAZI saves 50%+.

**Q: Is this for individuals or teams?**
A: Both. Our Starter tier is perfect for solo freelancers, while Business and Enterprise tiers support full team collaboration with roles, permissions, and shared workspaces.

**Q: What AI models are included?**
A: 12+ models including GPT-4o, Claude 3.5/3.7, Gemini, and specialized image generation models. Users can choose which AI to use for different tasks.
