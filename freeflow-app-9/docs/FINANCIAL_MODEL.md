**If growth is 50% slower AND burn is 20% higher:**
- Runway: 18 months (vs 27 months base)
- Action: Cut marketing spend, extend runway to 24 months
- Series A: Still achievable at M24 with $150K MRR

1. **Market Size:** $6.9B FMS market growing 14% CAGR
2. **Pricing Power:** AI features justify premium pricing
3. **Churn:** 4-5% monthly (industry standard for SMB)
4. **CAC Channels:** 40% paid, 30% content, 20% referral, 10% partnerships
