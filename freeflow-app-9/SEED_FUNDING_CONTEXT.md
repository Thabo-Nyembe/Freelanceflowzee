## Seed Funding Best Practices

### What Investors Prioritize (2025-2026)

Based on current research:

1. **Team (Most Important)**
   - Resilience and adaptability
   - Speed in processing new information
   - Previous startup experience (1.5-2x valuation premium)

2. **Traction**
   - Tangible metrics: "874 weekly active users" > vague claims
   - Pilot test results
   - Confirmed orders or waitlists

3. **Capital Efficiency**
   - Demonstrate resilience
   - Sustainable growth focus
   - Clear path to next milestone

4. **Market Opportunity**
   - Large TAM
   - Clear competitive advantage
   - Defensible position

**Source:** [Flowjam Seed Valuation Guide](https://www.flowjam.com/blog/seed-round-valuation-2025-complete-founders-guide)

### Pitch Deck Best Practices

A 10-slide deck that converts:

1. **Problem** (1 slide) - One-line problem statement
2. **Solution** (1 slide) - Target pain (who/why now)
3. **Product** (2 slides) - Unique solution with demo screenshots
4. **Evidence** (1 slide) - Pilots, waitlists, metrics
5. **Go-to-Market** (1 slide) - GTM wedge
6. **Unit Economics** (1 slide) - Key metrics
7. **Competition** (1 slide) - Edge over alternatives
8. **Team** (1 slide) - Credentials and experience
9. **Milestones** (1 slide) - 12-18 month plan
10. **The Ask** (1 slide) - Raise terms

**Source:** [Y Combinator Pitch Deck Guide](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/2u-how-to-build-your-seed-round-pitch-deck)

### Investor Targeting Strategy

Build a list of 60-120 targets ranked by:
- **Sector fit**: 25%+ portfolio in B2B
- **Stage**: Pre-seed/seed specialists
- **Check size**: $25K-$500K
- **Warm intro paths**: Network connections

**Key Insight**: 90% of rounds now use SAFEs for speed.

**Source:** [Metal SaaS AI Investors](https://www.metal.so/collections/top-25-seed-stage-saas-ai-investors-2025-deal-counts-check-sizes-warm-intros-metal)

### Financial Planning

- **Runway**: Raise for 24-30 months (18-month rule is now "dangerously optimistic")
- **Raise more than needed**: Market rewards patience
- **Average team size**: 6.2 employees at seed (down from 10.3 in 2021)
- **Geographic premium**: 66% of top valuations go to SF/NY startups

**Source:** [State of Seed 2025](https://developmentcorporate.com/startups/state-of-seed-2025-saas-founders/)
