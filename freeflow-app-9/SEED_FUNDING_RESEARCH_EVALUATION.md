## 3.2 Investor Priorities (2025-2026)

### What Investors Look For

| Priority | Weight | Evidence Required |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| **Team** | Highest | Experience, resilience, domain expertise |
| **Traction** | High | Metrics, pilots, waitlists |
| **Market** | High | TAM, growth rate, timing |
| **Product** | Medium | Demo, technology, differentiation |
| **Unit Economics** | Medium | LTV/CAC > 3:1, payback < 12 months |

### Red Flags That Deter Investors

1. Weak team dynamics or solo founder
2. No traction or pilot results
3. Unclear go-to-market strategy
4. Unrealistic projections
5. Heavy cash burn without path to profitability
6. Crowded market without differentiation

## 3.3 Pitch Deck Best Practices

### Optimal Structure (10 Slides)

| Slide | Content | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| 1 | One-line problem statement | 30 sec |
| 2 | Target pain (who/why now) | 1 min |
| 3-4 | Solution with product demo | 3 min |
| 5 | Early evidence (metrics) | 1 min |
| 6 | Go-to-market strategy | 1 min |
| 7 | Unit economics | 1 min |
| 8 | Competition edge | 1 min |
| 9 | Team credentials | 1 min |
| 10 | Milestones + The Ask | 1 min |

### Conversion Factors

- Lead with strengths (team experience or prototype)
- Use specific metrics ("874 weekly active users")
- Avoid vague claims ("huge market")
- Show 12-18 month milestones
- Include raise terms clearly

## 5.2 Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta (Months 1-3)
- **Target:** 100 beta users
- **Focus:** Product-market fit validation
- **Channels:** Direct outreach, freelancer communities
- **Metrics:** NPS, feature usage, retention

### Phase 2: Launch (Months 4-6)
- **Target:** 1,000 users
- **Focus:** Marketing foundation
- **Channels:** Content marketing, SEO, social media
- **Metrics:** MRR, CAC, conversion rate

### Phase 3: Growth (Months 7-12)
- **Target:** 5,000 users, $50K MRR
- **Focus:** Scale acquisition
- **Channels:** Paid ads, partnerships, referrals
- **Metrics:** LTV/CAC, growth rate, churn

### Phase 4: Scale (Months 13-18)
- **Target:** 10,000+ users, $150K MRR
- **Focus:** Series A readiness
- **Channels:** Enterprise sales, channel partners
- **Metrics:** ARR, net revenue retention
